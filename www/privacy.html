Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use WeatherPro Elite:

  • Location Data: We process the city names you enter to provide weather and flight information.
  • Contact Information: If you sign up for alerts, we collect your email address or phone number.
  • Usage Data: We store your recent search history (last 5 searches) in local sessions to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide weather forecasts and flight tracking data.
  • Send weather alerts via email or SMS as requested by you.
  • Personalize your experience through search history.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to power the app:

  • WeatherAPI: For weather data and news.
  • AviationStack: For flight information.
  • Twilio: For sending SMS alerts.
  • Google SMTP: For sending email alerts.

These services may collect their own data as governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the app easily by uninstalling it or ceasing to use it. You can also clear your search history by clearing your browser's session data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@weatherpro-elite.com.
